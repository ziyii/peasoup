How to Play

Roulette

Bet on a number and/or a colour and spin the wheel! Win if it lands on your pick!

Blackjack

Can you hit 21? Deal as many cards as possible until the sum equals 21. Aces can count as a 1 or an 11.

Slots

Pull that level and hope for triple 7s!
